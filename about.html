Architect Saulius Leliūga

Architecture is not only my profession, but also a hobby. I devote all my attention to each project to create and find the best solutions. My goal is to solve as many problems during the design process, so that everything goes smoothly in construction. I am constantly interested in the latest construction and finishing technologies to create architecture with long-term value. I have accumulated professional experience in designing various constructions in Lithuania and wooden construction hotels in the USA. I use the most advanced 3D modeling programs to design, avoiding human error. The main tool of work – Autodesk REVIT – specialized parametric design program based on information modeling technology (BIM). BIM technology allows you to build a 3D model in virtual time, analyze it in various incisions (structural, energy, lighting analysis, etc.) and use the data obtained to organize construction work and calculate the costs required for construction.
